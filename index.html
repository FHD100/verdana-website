Who we are

We are a multidisciplinary landscape company working from concept to construction to the life of the garden.

Verdana is dedicated to elevating outdoor environments through innovative design, quality construction, and sustainable maintenance. We approach every project with care, from site understanding and material selection to irrigation installation, planting, and long-term performance.
