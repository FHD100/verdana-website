01 Who we are

A multidisciplinary landscape partner from first sketch to living garden.

Verdana is a specialized landscaping and facility management company dedicated to elevating outdoor environments through innovative design, quality construction, and sustainable maintenance.

Every project is handled with care, from design and material selection to construction, irrigation installation, and long-term maintenance.

Concept layouts, master planning, 2D/3D visualization, plant palettes, and material selection.

02 Construction

Softscape, hardscape, irrigation systems, grading, outdoor lighting, and furniture placement.
